💡 How History Works
Step 1 — Enter your income and expenses on the Home page
Step 2 — Come to History → Preview Month → check the numbers look right
Step 3 — Click "Save to History" — stored permanently in data/history.csv
Step 4 — Next month, repeat! Old months stay saved even after server restarts.
Step 5 — Use the Compare tab to see how your spending changed month to month.
